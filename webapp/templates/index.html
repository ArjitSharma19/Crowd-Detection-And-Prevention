System Operator Guide

Detection Modes

  • Auto (Recommended): Dynamically switches models. It uses YOLO + SAHI for standard counting, and automatically shifts to CSRNet if crowd density or occlusions exceed safety margins.
  • YOLO: Fast, individual bounding box detection. Best for sparse/low crowds (up to 30 people) and real-time tracking (30+ FPS).
  • CSRNet: Highly accurate density map estimation. Best for dense/overlapping crowds (30+ people) or top-down angles (e.g. staircases, concert arenas).

View Modes

  • Raw box: Displays standard camera footage with blue bounding boxes drawn around detected individuals.
  • Heatmap: Shows continuous color-graded density mapping. Red/orange highlights indicate dangerous bottleneck areas (best used with CSRNet).
  • Grid cell: Overlays a 3x3 safety grid on the screen. Each cell lights up green, yellow, or red based on localized crowd capacity limits.
  • Motion flow: Renders direction vectors showing the average walking velocity of the crowd, useful for identifying escape bottlenecks.

Camera Environments

  • General: Configured for standard low-to-mid range security feeds or indoor room cameras.
  • Venue: Configured for large spaces like stadiums, ticket plazas, or corridors where wide coverage is needed.
  • Aerial: Optimized for overhead drone cameras or high-altitude security mounts looking straight down at tiny targets.
